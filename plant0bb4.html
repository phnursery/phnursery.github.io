English Boxwood

Sempervires 'Suffruiticosa'

Plant Type: Medium Boxwood

Max Size: 4' x 5'

Description: Often referred to as "Dwarf Boxwood." Used extensively in the Mid-Atlantic and in many of the colonial landscapes as an edging plant along walks or flower gardens. Optimum environment is dappled shade.
