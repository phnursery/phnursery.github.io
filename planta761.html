Winter Gem Boxwood

Microphylla var. Japonica

Plant Type: Large Boxwood

Max Size: 4' x 7'

Description: Is a Korean boxwood. Fast grower. Has an open habit. Tough and dependable boxwood. Has an annual new growth of 4'. Green Mound or Green Mountain could be a substitute for the Winter Gem.
