Grace Hendrick Phillips Boxwood

Microphylla

Plant Type: Dwarf Boxwood

Max Size: 1.5' x 3.5'

Description: Impressive dwarf mounding plant. Annual growth of 1/2" in height and 1 1/2" in width. Rich foliage. Used as specimen, edging or bonsai. Prefers shade. Is a favorite of ours for shaded areas.
