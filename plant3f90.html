Morris Dwarf Boxwood

Microphylla var. japonica

Plant Type: Dwarf Boxwood

Max Size: 1.5' x 2.5'

Description: Neat, compact mounding habit. Cluster of 2" brittle shoots result in a tufted bush outline. Used in bonsai and edging of dwarf gardens. Can tolerate some sun. Is a favorite of ours.
