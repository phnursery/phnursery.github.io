Justin Brouwers Boxwood

Sinica var. Insularis

Plant Type: Medium Boxwood

Max Size: 2.5' x 3.5'

Description: Excellent narrow dark green foliage on dwarf mounding plant. Similar to the English-Suffruiticosa in shape and growth, but ultimate size will be smaller. This is a favorite of ours.
