Graham Blandy Boxwood

Sempervirens

Plant Type: Upright Boxwood

Max Size: 8' x 1.5'

Description: Purely columnar grower. Often used as an accent plant, to frame a doorway or in areas where space is limited. Does not do well in poorly drained soil. Plant in shade to maintain its spectacular columnar habit.
