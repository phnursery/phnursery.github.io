Elegantissima Boxwood

Sempervirens

Plant Type: Medium Boxwood

Max Size: 5 1/2' x 4 1/2'

Description: One of the best variegated boxwood. Leaves have a creamy white edge. Avoid planting in areas susceptible to winter extremes because variety may be cold sensitive. Some shade tends to produce more attractive variegated color. Slow grower.
